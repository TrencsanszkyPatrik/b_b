Main page... or, how should we start?

The page's main use is a video editor. You can put our music(s) under a given video! Interested? Then let's get into it!

Once you're done with this, you just need to pick a song you like! Once you've found the perfect one, just click on it, and the whole process starts! Your finished video will be saved by the browser!
